About Rahmat Safety Industries

Pioneers in manufacturing safety materials with a focus on height safety and fall protection solutions

Our Commitment to Safety Excellence

Rahmat Safety Industries (RSI) takes immense pride in being an ISO 9001:2015 certified company dedicated to the field of Safety. RSI stands as a trusted and renowned brand in the industry, recognized as one of the fastest-growing companies globally in Fall Protection Products and Systems.

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Every safety product manufactured at RSI undergoes rigorous quality checks at multiple stages of production. Our team of highly experienced professionals maintains vigilant oversight to ensure each piece meets stringent global quality standards.

Global Reach

Our products are in high demand in the UK, Mexico, Middle East, Japan, Brazil, Hong Kong, Turkey, Poland, Spain, and more.

State-of-the-Art Facility

Our in-house production facility boasts cutting-edge machinery enabling us to produce top-quality products consistently.

Innovation Focus

We're committed to innovation with products like Retractable Fall Arrest Blocks and Fixed Line Systems aimed at providing the best in safety.
